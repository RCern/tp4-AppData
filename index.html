Bank Churners Visualisation

We have used the Bank Churners Dataset, this dataset is used to detect bank churners.

In our analysis we are looking more into the demographics of people that hold a Credit Card.

Card Type Distribution

Most clients hold the basic Blue Card.

Education Distribution

Most of the clients have a high-school level and graduate with very few holding a higher education level.

Gender Distribution

We can see that more females hold a card than males by a small margin.

Card Limit Distribution

We can see that most people have a low limit most of them having less than 4k$ limit.

Average Utilization Ratio

We can see that the average utilisation ratio is barely above 25%.

Income World Cloud

We can see that most people earn less than 40k$ per year.
